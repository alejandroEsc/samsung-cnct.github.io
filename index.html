About the Samsung SDS Cloud Native Computing Team

Samsung SDS provides frictionless software solutions to help you optimize productivity, make the right decisions, and strengthen your organization. The Samsung SDS Cloud Native Computing Team (CNCT) was created to advance this mission through research into practices that improve the velocity and efficiency of DevOps teams, within Samsung, for its clients and partners, and for the community.

As a technology team committed to invisible infrastructure, we bring insight into the requirements of modern web services delivered at scale. Samsung SDS has been a driving force behind Kubernetes and is committed to advancing containerized architectures. This work benefits Samsungâ€™s own deployments and execution in the hybrid cloud environment through improved automation, flexibility, co-tenancy, and efficiency.

Samsung SDS is committed to active participation in a vibrant open-source community that delivers production-grade infrastructure software. The CNCT contributes tools and research to advance cloud native innovation. These tools are designed to help you scale your infrastructure no matter where your services are deployed.

The CNCT also provides consulting to organizations making the shift to Cloud Native computing. Services include guidance refactoring development and operations groups, working with development teams to adopt technologies like Kubernetes, and performing SRE and (virtual) cluster operations services.
